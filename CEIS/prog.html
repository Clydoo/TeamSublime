The specialization in Software Engineering prepares students to become first-rate IT professionals, proficient in designing, developing and managing computing solutions. Specialization in Mobile and Web Applications

Students will gain a broad foundation on user interaction and global communication using digital technologies by specializing in Mobile and Web Applications. They will learn usability and design principles, software development and management, application development for cell phones, tablets, websites, handheld gadgets, and other devices. Further, students will be exposed to web design concepts and technologies, graphics and computer programming.
